Why qucchia? What does qucchia mean?

My internet name is based on the brand named Quechua. I just liked the sound of the name and one day, I came up with qucchia. Funnily enough, when I first came up with it, I thought it as the name of a community or website, not my own personal username.

Why doesn't qucchia start with an uppercase Q?

When I first created an account called qucchia, which was on Scratch, I accidentally made the q lowercase while I had been planning to make it uppercase. Since then, to stay consistent I suppose to my original name, I always try to make my username all lowercase. I won't complain if someone calls me "Qucchia", but I'll still consider it incorrect.
